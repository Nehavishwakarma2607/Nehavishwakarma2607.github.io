Stock Profit Calculator

Many retail investors lack the necessary tools to calculate their stock trading profitability efficiently. Manual calculations can be cumbersome, prone to errors, and may not account for factors such as transaction fees and taxes. The challenge is to develop a user-friendly application that allows users to input transaction details and instantly see their net profit or loss after accounting for all relevant costs.
